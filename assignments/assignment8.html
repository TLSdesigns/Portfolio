Elegance is Key

Definition:

Elegance is defined as ‘the quality of being graceful and stylish in appearance and manner.’

Therefore an elegant woman can be defined as someone who carries herself with poise in a sophisticated and fashionable way.

Having Class:

Having a sophisticated and classy style means that you can wear your outfits to work, Sunday brunch, a city shopping trip, or traveling the world. If you're trying to be elegant, start by wearing classic styles of clothing, like knee-length skirts and tailored button-down shirts, and choosing clothes made from high-quality fabrics, like silk and satin.
“Elegance is when the inside is as beautiful as the outside.” – Coco Chanel
