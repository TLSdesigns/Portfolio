Elegance is Key

Definition:

Elegance is defined as ‘the quality of being graceful and stylish in appearance and manner.’

Therefore an elegant woman can be defined as someone who carries herself with poise in a sophisticated and fashionable way.

Having Class:

Having a sophisticated and classy style means that you can wear your outfits to work, Sunday brunch, a city shopping trip, or traveling the world.
